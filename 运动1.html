臀桥  Hip Bridge

仰卧在瑜伽垫上，双腿屈曲略宽于肩，脚跟踩地发力将臀部抬起至大腿与身体呈一条直线，臀部抬起时上背部支撑地面下落时下背部贴地，但臀部悬空。

Lie on a yoga mat with your legs slightly wider than your shoulders and your feet flat on the ground. Push your heels into the ground to lift your hips up until your thighs and body form a straight line. When lifting the hips, support the upper back on the ground and keep the lower back close to the ground, but lift the hips off the ground.

锻炼肌肉：臀部

Muscles worked: Glutes

平板支撑  Plank

又称棒式、撑举、撑高、撑平板，是一种等长核心强度运动，会让身体维持在一个费力的姿势，且要维持相当一段时间。常见的平板支撑是前支撑，维持俯卧撑的姿势，身体重量由前臂、手肘及脚趾支持。

Also known as the stick pose, plank hold, high plank or front hold, is a type of isometric core strength exercise that involves maintaining a difficult position for a long period of time. The most common plank is the forearm plank, which involves holding the push-up position, with body weight supported by the forearms, elbows, and toes.

锻炼肌肉：核心及全身

Muscles worked: Core and entire body

深蹲  Squat

由站立姿势开始，通过起始时将臀部向后启动，屈膝和弯曲臀部，然后将躯干跟随负重下沉，接着回复到身体直立的姿势。深蹲可以有不同的深度，但是正确的深蹲大腿与地面至少是要平行的。平行位置以下的深蹲才被称为深蹲，而平行位置之上的只是浅蹲。 注意膝盖要沿着脚趾的方向，且膝盖不超过脚尖。

Starting from a standing position, initiate the movement by pushing your hips back, bending your knees, and flexing your hips. Then, lower your body down while keeping your torso upright and your weight on your heels. Return to a standing position by extending your hips and knees. A proper squat should have the thighs parallel to the ground or lower. Make sure to keep your knees aligned with your toes.

锻炼肌肉：臀部、大腿后部

Muscles worked: Glutes, hamstrings
