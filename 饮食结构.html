饮食结构

减肥时饮食的重点是吃饭规律、健康，而不是节食。

The key points of diet during weight loss are regularity and health, not dieting.

减肥的原理是摄入小于消耗，要想消耗快，必须摄入充足、均衡的食物，身体新陈代谢快，才能达到减肥效果。

The principle of weight loss is to have an intake less than expenditure. To achieve a faster expenditure, you must intake adequate and balanced food to promote body metabolism and achieve weight loss effects.

Try to eat less or avoid greasy and fried foods, and avoid snacking.

同时，要喝很多很多水。脂肪的代谢需要水分。在不伤害身体的前提下，每天喝较多的水，小口多次地喝水，也有利于减肥。

Also, drink a lot of water. Fat metabolism requires water. Drinking a lot of water every day in small sips can help with weight loss without harming your body.

在减肥的饮食上，不要太过难为自己，才能可持续。如果实在想吃点“垃圾食品”，也可以给自己放放假，但要记录好、控制好频次，例如每周1次奶茶。当然，吃完了也要相应加大运动量哦！

When it comes to dieting, don't be too hard on yourself in order to make it sustainable. If you really want to eat some "junk food," you can give yourself a break, but record and control the frequency, for example, once a week for bubble tea. Of course, you also need to increase the corresponding amount of exercise after eating.
